## Description 
TrailBuddy is a platform that takes away the hassle of going through many searches for an outdoor activity that may not even be what the user is looking for.
TrailBuddy's system supports users being able to curate their searches based on their likings and signing up for them on the same platform. TrailBuddy also supports providers to upload, update, and remove their listings as needed. TrailBuddy makes the process of exploring outdoor activities based on tailored needs, signing up, and leaving feedback a whole lot easier.
